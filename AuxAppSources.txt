** AUXILIARY PROGRAMS USED **
This is a list of the different applications used to complete this project.

Cronie – Used as the method to add my scripts to the start-up.
Memtester – Used in the MemoryTest.sh script.
SpeedTest-CLI – Used in the NetworkTest.sh script.
LSHW – Used in the SystemInfo.sh script.
Nano – Used in the TextEdit.sh script.
ClamAV – Used in the VirusScan.sh script.
